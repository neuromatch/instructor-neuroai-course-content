Prerequisite knowledge#

The additional notions you should be comfortable with while exploring todays tutorials are Fourier Transform and Convolution. We strongly recommend you look at the following video resources by Three Blue One Brown (3b1b) on these concepts if you dont feel confident about them (Fourier Transform, Convolution). Familiarizing with them will benefit your intuition behind the proposed materials here.
